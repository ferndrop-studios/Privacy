## What this app does

This app is a card collecting companion tool designed to help users track and manage lists of trading cards.

Users can organise their personal card lists.

---

## Information we collect

We try to collect as little personal information as possible.

---

## Information stored on your device

This app stores data locally on your device so it can function properly.

This may include:

- card lists and wishlist items  
- custom list names and organisation  
- app preferences and settings  

This information is stored only on your device.

---

## Advertising data

If ads are shown in the app, we use **Google AdMob** to display them.

AdMob may collect or receive information such as:

- device identifiers  
- IP address  
- approximate location  
- app usage information  
- ad performance and interaction data  

This information may be used by Google and its partners to provide, improve, and measure ads.

You can read more about how Google uses data here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

---

## Network requests

This app may connect to external services to retrieve card data or pricing information.

These requests do not include personal user data and are used only to display relevant information within the app.

---

## Information we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally ask for or collect:

- your name  
- your home address  
- your phone number  
- your contacts  
- your photos  
- your microphone recordings  
- your camera data  

unless a future version of the app clearly tells you otherwise.

---

## How we use information

We use information to:

- make the app function correctly  
- store and display your card lists  
- improve app stability and performance  
- show ads, if ads are enabled

## Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information.
